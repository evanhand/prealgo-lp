## How to pick yours

<Steps>
  <Step n="01" title="Match the archetype to the video">
    If it's a transformation, use Magician. If it's a teardown, use Investigator. Forcing a Contrarian hook on a tutorial video where it doesn't fit is worse than a clean Teacher hook.
  </Step>
  <Step n="02" title="Check what your audience expects">
    If you've established a Story-hook personality, switching to Contrarian without warning confuses subscribers and tanks engagement.
  </Step>
  <Step n="03" title="Calibrate to platform">
    Same content, different platform sometimes wants a different hook. Instagram rewards Story + Magician; TikTok rewards Contrarian; YouTube Shorts rewards Teacher.
  </Step>
</Steps>
